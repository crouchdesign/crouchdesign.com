Making new and interesting art is all about finding the right inspiration. That’s why I’ll never leave without a sketchbook. I never know when I will get inspired. My inspiration is everywhere; graffiti, dreams, quotes, everything. I scribble it out or paste the pictures on the blank pages, and suddenly, my ideas begin to take shape. Once I have gathered everything, I dump out the ideas, spread out the pages and begin to work. Among the muddled mess that is my sketches, I will succeed to create something simple, clean and straight to the point. Graphics are my medium, and drawing from existing insight, I make refreshing and new ideas that will help others get inspired.
